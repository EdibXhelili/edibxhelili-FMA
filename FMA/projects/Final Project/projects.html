How to make a simple cake!

  • The first thing you need to do is go and
    buy 6 eggs and sugar. After that mix them together
    well for about 10 - 15 minutes.

  • After that make the oven ready by pre heating
    it on 150* celcius. Prepare the chocolate that you
    are gonna add on the cake.

  • Then finaly bake the cake, add the chocolate,
    shape the pieces of the cake and let it cool. Thats all
    you need to do for an amazing simple cake

How to drive a car!

  • Sit in the drivers seat, adjust the mirrors
    and seat, fasten your seatbelt, and start the engine.

  • Shift into drive, gently press the accelerator,
    and keep both hands on the steering wheel while staying aware of your surroundings.

  • Slow down, use your turn signals, and safely
    park by shifting into park and turning off the engine.

How to grow a plant!

  • Choose a suitable pot and fill it with nutrient-rich soil.

  • Plant the seeds or a young plant, then water it gently to moisten the soil.

  • Place the plant in a spot with enough sunlight, water regularly, and watch it grow!
